Our Services

We offer friendly solutions that engages and empowers disadvantaged teenagers and young people to access livelihood and life skills to realize their potential through the following thematic areas;

Education and Youth Skills Development

This focus area involves initiatives aimed at improving access to vocational skills for young people and equipping them with the necessary skills for personal and professional growth. This includes programs such as vocational training, mentorship, and educational opportunities.

Adolescent Health

iKitty Life addresses issues related to the physical, mental, and emotional well-being of adolescents. This involves programs focused on sexual and reproductive health, mental health awareness, nutrition and substance abuse prevention for young people.

Youth Livelihood

This thematic area involves initiatives aimed at promoting economic empowerment among young people. It includes entrepreneurship programs, job training, financial literacy education, microfinance opportunities, and support for small business development.

Youth-Led Advocacy

iKitty Life seems to prioritize empowering young people to advocate for their rights and interests. This involves training youth in advocacy skills, supporting youth-led organizations and movements, promoting youth participation in decision-making processes, and advocating for policy changes that benefit young people.
