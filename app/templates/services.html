Our Services

We offer friendly solutions that engages and empowers disadvantaged teenagers and young people to access support on menstrual hygiene management, SRHR and access to safe contraceptives through;

1-hour Girls Empowerment Class

We deliver evidence-based training for girls on Sexual Reproductive Health with one powerful message to avoid older men. We aim to reach over 1 million teens, young men and women in Uganda. We conduct randomized field trials to trigger learning and retain our freshness in our works.

Teen-to-Teens Program

This program empowers teenagers themselves to become agents of change in their communities. Teens are recruited, trained and are matched with fellow peers to provide mentoring support on leadership, sexual reproductive health and right education.

Menstrual Hygiene & Supplies

iKitty Life builds last mile network of teenage girls who empower their peers in menstrual hygiene management and affordable access to menstrual supplies.

Contraceptives for Teens

iKitty Life has a model where teenagers are at the centre of last mile distribution cycle of contraceptive where 1) teenagers to get unhindered access to contraceptive 2) it is also a source of income for teens through commissions.
